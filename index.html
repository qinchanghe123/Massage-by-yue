Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive?
Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment to fill out a brief intake form and begin relaxing.
Can I get a massage if I have an injury?
Yes, but please inform your therapist beforehand. In some cases, massage can aid recovery, but we may need to avoid specific inflamed areas.
What is your cancellation policy?
We respectfully request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.
Is parking available?
Yes, we have ample free parking right in front of our storefront for your convenience.
Do you accept insurance?
We do not accept insurance directly, but we can provide a receipt for you to submit to your provider for reimbursement if your plan covers massage therapy.
